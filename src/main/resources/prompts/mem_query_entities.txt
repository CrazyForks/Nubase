  person | location | organization | product | event | food | date | other

Output schema (STRICT JSON, no markdown):
{"entities": [{"text": "<surface form>", "type": "<type>"}, ...]}

Rules:
- Output JSON only. No prose, no markdown fences.
- Common nouns and generic words are NOT entities. Skip them.
- Pronouns ("he", "it", "they") are NOT entities. Skip them.
- If there are no entities, return {"entities": []}.

Examples:

Query: who is John?
Output: {"entities": [{"text":"John","type":"person"}]}

Query: my favourite food
Output: {"entities": []}

Query: what did I say about the Atlas launch in Tokyo last week?
